Privacy Policy for Zhoplist

Last updated: September 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Zhoplist is a simple shopping list app that helps you organize your purchases. We care about your privacy and this policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Information We Collect

Shopping Lists Data: We store your shopping list items, including titles, descriptions, categories, priorities, and completion status on our servers to enable synchronization across devices.

Session Identifiers: We generate and store unique session IDs (either automatically generated codes or custom list codes you create) to keep your shopping lists separate from other users.

Timestamps: We record when items are created and modified for proper synchronization.

No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personally identifiable information.

3. How We Use Information

We use your data solely to provide the shopping list functionality, including:

  • Storing and synchronizing your shopping lists across devices
  • Enabling shared shopping lists through custom list codes
  • Maintaining data integrity and preventing data loss

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share your shopping list data with third parties. Your data is only accessible to you through your session ID or custom list code.

5. Security

We implement security measures to protect your data, including secure server storage and session-based access control. Your shopping lists are only accessible through your unique session ID.

6. Data Retention

Your shopping list data is retained on our servers to provide continuous service. You can delete individual items or your entire shopping list at any time through the app.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your shopping list data at any time
  • Delete individual items or your entire shopping list
  • Create custom list codes for sharing lists with others

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
